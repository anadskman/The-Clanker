## **CAD/PCB Models**



#### **PCB Models**



I installed a keyboard switch models plugin in the plugin manager.

This meant in the pcb I can select each switch and set its switch and stablisers and it would be auto aligned.



Once all the models were on the board except the keys, I exported everything and uploaded the step to fusion and started the case.



I finished placing all the keycaps and then i decided to make the keys part of the case lower than the rest.



Once I finished the basics of the case I cut the keyboard in half as it is too big for my 3d printer.

I just made a mid plane and cut the board in half and added 9x9mm square top and bottom for the left and right sides to be glued together.

The mounting holes would also hold them together going through the pcb.



Lastly I finished exporting everything and uploaded everything to GitHub.
